    Few Words About Us

    We are a Virtual Airline that follow the real-world Neos Air operations. Neos Air is the 2nd largest Italian airline and its main hub is in Milan Malpensa. Our VA is hosted on the vAMSYS platform and we use P3D, MSFS and XPlane. We also use online networks including VATSIM and IVAO.

    Our mission is bold but clear. We aim to transform the virtual realm into an increasingly engaging, inclusive, and astonishing environment. We are committed to creating experiences that ignite imagination and connect people in innovative ways.

    Our top priority is to provide virtual experiences of the highest quality. Every detail is carefully crafted to ensure that each experience is exceptional. Whether you are looking for entertainment, education, or anything else, we are here to bring your virtual dream to life.

    Our Destinations

    Explore the world with vNEOS, the virtual airline that connects you to extraordinary destinations around the globe. Our extensive flight network offers opportunities for every taste, whether you're seeking thrilling adventures, captivating cultures, or simply relaxation in tropical paradises.

    Among our primary destinations that closely mirror the real world Neos air, in addition to European ones, are the Caribbean Islands, with dreamy beaches and crystal-clear waters. You can also choose to explore North America, with fascinating destinations like the dynamic cities of the USA, including New York, Miami, and Los Angeles. Alternatively, discover the natural beauty of Canada in Toronto and Vancouver. If you're looking for a unique experience, immerse yourself in the distinctive atmosphere of Africa with destinations such as Zanzibar, Mombasa, and Nosy Be. Here, you can uncover the rich African culture and the majesty of wildlife. And don't forget Asia, with its places steeped in history and charm like Bangkok, Phuket, and Colombo, or the modern Japanese metropolises of Tokyo and Osaka.
